Once upon a time, they lived a lion and a mouse. One day, lion was sleeping. The mouse started playing on it. Lion woke up. He caught the mouse and was going to kill. The mouse requested for forgiveness. The lion let him go. After some days, lion caught in a net. Mouse started chewing the ropes. Soon, the lion was free. He thanked the mouse. Moral. Small acts of kindness will be rewarded greatly.
